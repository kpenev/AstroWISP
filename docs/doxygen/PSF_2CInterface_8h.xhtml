Evaluate a PSF/PRF at a collection of offsets from the source center.

Parameters
psfThe PSF/PRF to evaluate.
x_offsetsThe offsets from the source center in the x direction of the points to evaluate the PSF at. Must have a size equal to num_points.
y_offsetsThe offsets from the source center in the y direction of the points to evaluate the PSF at. Must have a size equal to num_points.
num_pointsThe number of locations we are evaluating the PSF/PRF at.
resultThe location to fill with the values of the PSF/PRF. Must already be allocated with a size of num_points.

See PSF::PSF::integrate, but calculates multiple integrals.

Parameters
psfThe PSF to integrate.
center_xThe x coordinates of the centers of the rectangles to integrate over.
center_yThe y coordinates of the centers of the rectangles to integrate over.
dxThe widths of the rectangles.
dyThe heights of the rectangles.
circle_radiiThe radii of the circles. For zero entries, the integral is over the full rectangle.
num_integralsThe number of integrations requested.
resultThe location to fill with the values of the calculated integrals. Must already be allocated with a size of num_integrals.
